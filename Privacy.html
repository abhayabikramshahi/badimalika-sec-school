Privacy Policy

Safeguarding your information is our priority.

1. Information Collection

We collect the following types of information to provide the best educational services:

  • Student Data: Includes name, date of birth, address, academic performance, and health information.
  • Parent/Guardian Data: Contact details, occupation, and socioeconomic information.
  • Staff Data: Professional qualifications, employment history, and contact details.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

  • Develop effective curricula tailored to student needs.
  • Ensure student health, safety, and welfare.
  • Facilitate communication with parents and guardians.
  • Comply with government reporting requirements.

3. Data Security

To protect your personal information, we implement:

  • Secure storage of records (both physical and digital).
  • Restricted access to authorized personnel only.
  • Data encryption for sensitive information.
  • Regular audits of data security practices.

4. Stakeholder Rights

  • Access: Request access to your personal data.
  • Correction: Seek corrections for inaccuracies.
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for specific data usage.
  • Complaints: Lodge complaints with the school's Data Protection Officer.
