About Us

Discover the legacy and vision of Badimalika Secondary School

Welcome to Badimalika Secondary School

Established in 2032 B.S., Badimalika Secondary School has been a beacon of academic excellence and holistic development in Nepal. Located amidst the serene surroundings of Kalikot, our school has nurtured thousands of young minds with dedication and integrity.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students to achieve academic success and develop essential life skills, preparing them for a bright and prosperous future.

Our Vision

We aim to create a nurturing environment where students can excel academically, socially, and emotionally, fostering a culture of lifelong learning and leadership.

Our Core Values

Excellence

Striving for academic and personal excellence in every aspect.

Integrity

Fostering honesty, respect, and strong moral principles.

Community

Building a sense of belonging and responsibility among students.
