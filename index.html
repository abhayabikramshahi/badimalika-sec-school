Badimalika Secondary School

Message From Principal

"Being the principal of Badimalika, my mission is inspired by the desire to offer quality, inclusive education to all children, particularly the vulnerable ones. I work with the aim of making an English-Medium educational facility that will serve students from my district and outside the district. Partnering with NGOs, we have helped reduce dropout rates and return students to school by addressing issues such as child marriage and poverty. I want parents to appreciate the value of education in the life of a child and use it for the benefit of the child’s future. All children have a right to learn, and that’s what I stand for."
