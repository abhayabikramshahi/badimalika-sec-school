About Us

Badimalika Secondary School, Raskot Municipality, Kalikot, Nepal, is one of the renowned community schools that offers a range of academic programs. The school runs 10+2 level programs in disciplines such as Education, Humanities, Management, Agriculture, and Animal Science and is accredited by the NEB and the Government of Nepal, Ministry of Education. It adopts an inclusive, child-friendly approach to education, and scholarships are made available to students on merit or quota basis.

In recent times, Badimalika Secondary School has recorded a high enrollment of students, especially with initiatives such as free enrollment, free textbooks, and midday meal programs. Collaborative efforts with organizations such as Plan International Nepal, together with local government campaigns, have also raised awareness of education, reduced dropout rates, and addressed child marriage and other forms of discrimination. The school currently has an enrollment of more than 1,199 students, up from previous years.
